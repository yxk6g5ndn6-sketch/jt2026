Tokyo in November — What to Expect
🌡️10–17°CTemperature
🍂Late AutumnSeason
☀️Crisp & DryConditions
☂️LowRain Chance

November is one of the best times to visit Tokyo — cool, largely dry, and still beautiful as the autumn colour winds down. Mornings and evenings drop to around 8–10°C, so a proper jacket is worthwhile. Afternoons are mild and comfortable.
