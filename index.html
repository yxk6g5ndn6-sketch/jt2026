🍂   Tokyo in November — What to Expect
🌡️10–17°CTemperature
☀️Crisp & ClearConditions
🍁Peak AutumnFoliage

November is one of the best times to be in Tokyo — cool, dry, and absolutely beautiful with the autumn leaves. Mornings and evenings get cold (8–10°C), so bring a proper jacket. Afternoons are lovely. Pack layers you can add and remove easily.
